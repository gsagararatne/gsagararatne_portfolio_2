Call me “Dra”

Came to Canada as an international student for university. Picked mechatronics engineering because it was the jack of all trades. Worked as a research assistant for 20 months during uni and got to detect early signs of cancer through nano particles. Little did I know that I was incorporating Machine Learning techniques already. For me, it was practically statistics on top of the statistical analysis that I was doing.

After graduating with my B.Eng with 20 months of research experience, I did a Data Science diploma at BrainStation to further learn tools, visualisation techniques, supervised and unsupervised machine learning concepts, version control and big data manipulation to ease into my transition as a data scientist.

What I like to do when I’m not coding behind a screen:

Powerlifting. I was a collegiate athlete that represented Ryerson University at provincial and collegiate level powerlifting competitions.























Cooking. I’ve been cooking a lot more during the pandemic. Always down to share recipes. Bonus if they’re instant pot recipes.

























Pottery. A new hobby I picked up in September. Hope to continue it this Spring/Summer.














Going on trails. Hope to eventually go on hikes in different provinces.















Attending local concerts. As a bassist, I enjoy seeing local live bands perform!
