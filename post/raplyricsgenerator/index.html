Rap Lyrics Generator

29/12/2021 One-minute read

Deployed a rap lyrics generator on streamlit by hosting trained RNN model on an AWS S3 bucket.

Streamlit Demo


Backstory

I was catching up with a friend from Bahrain over a video call sometime in November 2021. That’s when we brought up about how we used to share earphones and listen to rap music together on his mp3 on the school bus back home. That got us talking about our current taste in music and our appreciation for lyrical rap. That’s when I thought of building a rap lyrics generator achieved through a Recurrent Neural Network.

Project Summary

  • Extracted 2259 rap songs through GeniusAPI and created a rap lyrics generator by training a RNN (TensorFlow) model
  • Locally deployed system as web application through streamlit while accessing trained model from an AWS S3 bucket
